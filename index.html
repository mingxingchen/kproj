Welcome to kproj

kproj is a code to perform band unfolding calculations for solid states based on the k-projection method. Currently, it is interfaced to VASP. People who are interested in this program can obtain it by writing to Mingxing Chen (chen59@uwm.edu)

Selected features

  • •Unfolded band structure for bulk and interfaces 

  • •Vacuum-weighted bands 

  • • Effect of polarization on ARPES spectrum
